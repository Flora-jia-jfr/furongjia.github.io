> Submitted to [EAAI-24](https://eaai-conf.github.io/index.html)

Time series forecasting is an essential area of machine learn- ing with a wide range of real-world applications. Most of the previous forecasting models aim to capture dynamic charac- teristics from uni-modal numerical historical data. Although extra knowledge can boost the time series forecasting perfor- mance, it is hard to collect such information. In addition, how to fuse the multi-modality information is non-trivial. In this paper, we first propose a general principle of collecting the corresponding textual information from different data sources with the help of modern large language models (LLM). Then, we propose a prompt-based LLM framework to utilize both the numerical data and the textual information simultane- ously, named GPT4MTS. In practice, we propose a GDELT- based multimodal time series dataset for news number fore- casting, which provides a concise and well-structured ver- sion of time series dataset with textual information for further research in communication. Through extensive experiments, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our proposed method on forecasting tasks with extra-textual information. In addition, we apply a case study on different news events of two coun- tries for a better understanding of our proposed dataset.
